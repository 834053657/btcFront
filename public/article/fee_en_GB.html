Fees

Bitcoin transactions

Registering, buying, and selling bitcoins is completely free.

Each utomarket user who creates a trading ad needs to pay a 1% fee for each completed transaction. (Currently create transactional ads free of charge)


Bitcoin transaction fee

Trading with other utomarket users' wallets is free.

Trading with other Bitcoin wallets requires a Bitcoin network transfer fee. The current fee can be seen on the wallet page under the title bitcoin transfer fee. How much the fee will change depends on the current usage rate of the Bitcoin blockchain. We will automatically adjust this fee based on the current network congestion to ensure that your transaction can be confirmed as soon as possible. After you send the transaction, the transaction fee will be deducted from your utomarket wallet.

When funds are sent to the utomarket wallet, the Bitcoin network transfer fee will be deducted from the deposit. Fees vary according to the bitcoin network congestion situation. If your deposit amount is less than the bitcoin deposit fee, this deposit will not be processed. You can see the current cost of sending funds below when sending Bitcoins on the wallet page.
