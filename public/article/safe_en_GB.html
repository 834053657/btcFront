How to improve my account security?

Here are a few tips to protect you from most fishing and hacking threats.

1. Enable two-factor authentication for your account. This protects your account when a third party cracks your password. After you enable two-factor authentication, you only know that the password will not be able to log in to your account. Be sure to note the backup code, preferably on paper, in case the code is lost.

2. Do not reuse your password on other websites or send passwords to other people or even your friends.

3. Do not use an e-mail provider that requires a password, such as yopmail, as this may access your account without having to know the password. If you want a secure, anonymous email, use services like Tutanota.

You can enable all these security features in the personal center's account settings;


For Bitcoin, the owner of the wallet and Bitcoin is responsible for his/her own security. Since bitcoin transactions are irreversible, if your user account is stolen, Bitcoin will be lost and utomarket will not be able to help you retrieve them. Therefore, it is important to follow the above safety instructions.



What should I do if someone else visits my account?

• Change your password immediately

• Contact customer service immediately to explain what happened and your account name, email used to register utomarket, name, IP address, phone number associated with the account, and your recent activity on the site. You can find the IP address by searching Google for "What is my IP?".



Two-factor authentication

Use high security login to protect your Bitcoin.



What is two-factorauthentication?

• To login, in addition to your password, you need a one-time code that you can get from your phone or print on paper.

• Utomarket two-factor authentication can be used for mobile app (Android, iOS, Windows Mobile) or printed paper codes.



Why use two-factor authentication?

• More than 99% of hacks can be prevented through two-factor authentication

• Unlike passwords, two-factor authentication codes are hard to be stealen.

• It takes only a few minutes to setup.



Other measures of safety

Do not use the same password on different websites.

Do not use the Tor browser. Using Tor Browser will increases the risk of Bitcoin being stolen.

Do not participate in transactions without utomarket.

Do not use shared computers or devices, such as those in public internet cafes, because they may have a keylogger installed to steal your user credentials.

When login the website, pay attention to the browser's address bar to make sure that you are actually logged into www.utomarket.com instead of the phishing link. Please confirm the spelling is www.utomarket.com because phishers, especially e-mail phishers, often register domain names like www.utomarket.com.

Please try to access the Bitcoin wallet from a computer dedicated to financial tasks. Do not use this computer for other tasks.

Do not install third-party software, pirated software, or browser plug-ins that you cannot trust 100 percent. This can greatly reduce the risk of computer-infected malware stealing Bitcoin.



How to enable two-factor authentication for my account?

Two-factor authentication can be enabled by the account setting. We only provide two-factor authentication for mobile devices. We recommend that you use mobile device-based two-factor authentication and use paper-based two-factor authentication only if you cannot use your smartphone.

When two-factor authentication is activated, the backup code needs to be recorded in a safe location, preferably on paper. If you can't find a two-factor authentication code, you won't be able to login. You need to contact customer service and provide the appropriate information to reset.


How to activate mobile device-based two-factor authentication

1. Click to enable two-factor verification

2. Install the authentication app on your phone. Google Authenticator works on Android, iPhone, iPad, iPod, and Windows Phone. You can find it in the app store and download it.

3. Record the backup code shown on the following image page and record it in a safe place, preferably on paper. this is very important.

4. Launch Google Authenticator on your phone and push BEGIN.

5. Select the scana barcode in the app.

6. Allows the app to take pictures and videos if required by the app. Then, you can scan the QR code displayed on the utomarket website.

7. Now the two-factor authentication code will appear in the Google Authenticator app. To complete the setup, enter the code from the Google Authenticator app into the Google verification code on the utomarket website and click I have backed up my Secret key, and then click Enabled.

Now You have completed all the settings and will receive double authentication protection! When you login your account, you will be asked to provide the code shown in the authentication app.
